1. List the product names, only include those purchased quantity more than 3.
mysql> SELECT pname FROM Product WHERE pid IN (SELECT pid FROM Invoice_item WHERE quantity > 3);

3. List the names of all customers who have purchased at least 2 products, making sure that no duplicate names are in the result.

mysql> SELECT DISTINCT name FROM Customer WHERE id IN (SELECT cust_id FROM INVOICE_MASTER WHERE inv_id IN (SELECT inv_id FROM Invoice_item GROUP BY inv_id HAVING COUNT(pid) >= 2));
+-------+
| name  |
+-------+

+-------+
